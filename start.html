Before you start

We may not be able to process your appeal until you've sent your Self Assessment tax return.

For guidance on what a reasonable excuse, see what may count as a resonable excuse.

You will be asked to give us:

  • that date the penalty was issued
  • the date you filed your Self Assessment tax return, if you've filed it
  • details of the reasonable excuse for late filing

If you don't think you should be in Self Assessment, check if you need to fill in a Self Assessment tax return.
